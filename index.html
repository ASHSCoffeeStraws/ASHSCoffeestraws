    Coffee History

    The below video is a documentary about Coffee. But why have we made this such a prominent feature on our website?

    We talk about Coffee a lot and have added in this video to show you about the journey it takes to be created. From the fields and farms in Columbia to your local Starbucks in New Zealand. It is a massive process of transfering the coffee bean from the farm simply to your straw to get the roasted flavour of the bean itself. The way it's harvested, shipped, aerated, roasted, and then how it's turned into whats within your straw is all a process that has been refined over many years to be accessible for you.
